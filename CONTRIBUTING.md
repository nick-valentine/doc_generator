# Contributing to Self-Documenting Go Parser with Call Graphs

Thank you for your interest in this project!

## ⚠️ PRs and Issue Merging Policy

Please note: **This is a personal, highly customized, AI-slop-driven codebase.** 

As a result:
* **Pull Requests (PRs) will NOT be merged.**
* **Issues will not be addressed or triaged.**

The codebase is published primarily as a reference, a public demonstration of dynamic Go plugins coupled with Tree-Sitter grammars, and for backup/collaboration with our personal AI partner.

## 🍴 Fork and Build Your Own!

If you want to add features, fix bugs, or adapt this tool to your own workflow:
1. **Fork the repository** directly on GitHub.
2. Clone your fork locally.
3. Hack away, author new plugins, change the generators, and build your own dream visualization tools!

We encourage you to use this as a reference or a springboard for your own awesome development tools! Happy coding!
